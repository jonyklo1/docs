## What's new

<Update label="2025-09-21" description="Robots released" tags={["Robots", "New feature"]}>
  Robots are now available to automate searches, run follow‑up research questions for each place, and optionally email results.

  - Create robots from the Filters sidebar
  - Schedule searches with flexible cadences
  - Ask follow‑up research questions per result
  - Export and optionally email new leads after runs

  See the [Robots guide](/user-guide/robots) for details.
</Update>
